Big Data - The Revolutionary Road

Big Data is the latest buzzword. Everything that you see around you thrives on data, and its facets. Every action that you take, expenditure you make, and the income you receive, all get filed into a massive database, accessible by the ones who wish to analyse the same. These structured or unstructured data are often mined, extracted, and utilised in different forms, in order to rationalise decisions and devise algorithms that would enhance human life.
